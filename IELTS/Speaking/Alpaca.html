IELTS Speaking Topics

每个单元按 Part 2 和 Part 3 上下展示。

Unit 1

一家新开的店

Part 2

Describe a new shop that has recently opened in your city/town.

You should say:

  • Where the shop is
  • What it sells
  • Who goes to this shop
  • And explain how successful you think it will be in the future

Part 3

  1. What kinds of shops are popular in your city?
  2. Why do young people enjoy going to some boutiques?
  3. Do you think location is the key factor that attracts customers?
  4. What is the difference between a small shop and a large shop?
  5. How important are price and quality in influencing consumer behaviour?
  6. Why is cheap fashion so popular?
Unit 3

3. 鼓励别人做不愿做的事

Part 2

Describe a time when you encouraged someone to do something that he/she didn't want to do.

You should say:

  • Who he or she is
  • What you encouraged him/her to do
  • How he/she reacted
  • And explain why you encouraged him/her to do it

Part 3

  1. How can leaders encourage their employees?
  2. What kind of encouragement should parents give?
  3. When should parents encourage their children?
  4. Do you think people are better than others at persuading?
  5. Should children do everything their parents are ask them to do?
  6. How can employers encourage their staff?
